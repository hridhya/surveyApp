SURVEY MEDDLE



This is a Survey app. Survey is aimed completely for crowd based audio/video detection. Once you sign-up, you will be taken to a home page where any number of surveys can be created. As many as 10 audio/video links can be posted in a single survey. Once a survey is submitted, you will be given a url which could be send to as many people you like. They can participate without needing to sign-up, annonymously. The results of a survey can be viewed in detail on your home page.
